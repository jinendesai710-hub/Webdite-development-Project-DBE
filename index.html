Our Story

MORE THAN
A GYM

Founded in 2023, The Body Project Gym was built on one belief: that transformational fitness should be accessible, science-backed, and deeply human. We are not a franchise. We are a community.

Our facility in the heart of Sion, Mumbai houses cutting-edge equipment, recovery zones, and programming designed by certified performance specialists who understand that your body is your most important project.

Whether you’re stepping into a gym for the first time or working towards your peak performance — this is your arena.
